About

Health-in-a-box is an initiative of the Federal Ministry of Health to provide a central information system for tertiary healthcare institutions across the country.

Learn More

National Health Informatics center

Seated at Federal Ministry of Health, the National Health Informatics Dashboard provides real-time notifications on epidemics, disease patterns, prescription count hike, and financial patterns/behaviour, etc within the hospitals.

Learn More

Hospital Management Information System

The Hospital Management Information System (HMIS) provides end-to-end information across the national health system geared towards improving the efficiency, effectiveness, and quality of services at different levels of health delivery.

Learn More

National Telemedecine Framework

Leveraging on this, remote telemedicine infrastructure is being layered to provide a framework for veritable and controlled implementation of telemedicine across the national health system.

Learn More
